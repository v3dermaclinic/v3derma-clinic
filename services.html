Our Services

Comprehensive Skin, Hair & Aesthetic Treatments

🩺 Clinical Dermatology

Expert diagnosis and treatment for all skin conditions

Acne Treatment

Comprehensive treatment for all grades of acne — from mild comedonal acne to severe cystic acne — using topical therapy, oral medications and clinical procedures.

Psoriasis Management

Long-term management plans for psoriasis including topical treatments, phototherapy and systemic therapies tailored to disease severity.

Eczema & Dermatitis

Treatment for atopic dermatitis, contact dermatitis and other forms of eczema with a focus on skin barrier restoration and itch control.

Vitiligo

Advanced vitiligo management including phototherapy, topical immunomodulators and surgical options like melanocyte transfer.

Fungal Infections

Diagnosis and treatment of ringworm, candidiasis, nail fungus and other fungal skin infections with targeted antifungal therapy.

Warts & Moles

Safe removal of warts, moles, skin tags and other benign growths using cryotherapy, electrocautery and radiofrequency methods.

Allergies & Urticaria

Identification and management of skin allergies, hives and chronic urticaria with allergy testing and preventive strategies.

Pigmentation Disorders

Treatment for melasma, post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation, dark circles and uneven skin tone using peels, lasers and topical agents.

💇 Hair Treatments

Restore hair health and combat hair loss with proven therapies

PRP Hair Therapy

Platelet-rich plasma injections to stimulate dormant hair follicles, promote natural growth and reduce thinning.

Hair Fall Treatment

Comprehensive evaluation and multi-pronged treatment for hair fall including medications, supplements and procedural therapies.

Dandruff & Scalp Care

Targeted treatment for dandruff, seborrheic dermatitis, scalp psoriasis and itchy scalp conditions.

Alopecia Treatment

Management of alopecia areata, androgenetic alopecia and other forms of hair loss with advanced medical and procedural options.

✨ Laser Treatments

Precision laser therapy for fast, visible results

Laser Hair Reduction

Permanent hair reduction for face, underarms, bikini line and full body using US-FDA approved diode laser technology.

Laser Scar Removal

Fractional CO2 and erbium laser treatments for acne scars, surgical scars, burn scars and stretch marks.

Laser Skin Resurfacing

Improve skin texture, reduce pores and achieve smoother skin with advanced laser resurfacing procedures.

Laser Pigmentation Correction

Targeted laser therapy for sun spots, age spots, freckles and stubborn pigmentation that doesn't respond to creams.

Tattoo Removal

Safe, effective laser tattoo removal with Q-switched Nd:YAG laser technology for all ink colours.

Laser for Birthmarks

Treatment of port-wine stains, haemangiomas and other vascular birthmarks using pulsed dye laser.

💎 Cosmetic & Aesthetic Treatments

Rejuvenate, restore and enhance your natural beauty

Chemical Peels

Medical-grade peels (glycolic, salicylic, TCA, Jessner's) for acne, pigmentation, dullness and anti-aging.

Anti-Aging Treatments

Comprehensive anti-aging solutions including retinoid therapy, growth factor treatments and collagen-boosting procedures.

Dermal Fillers

Hyaluronic acid fillers for lip enhancement, cheek volumisation, under-eye hollows and nasolabial folds.

Micro-Needling / MNRF

Microneedling radiofrequency for acne scars, open pores, fine lines and skin rejuvenation with minimal downtime.

HydraFacial

Multi-step facial treatment that cleanses, exfoliates, extracts and hydrates for an instant skin glow.

Skin Brightening

Customised brightening protocols using glutathione, vitamin C infusions and combination therapies for radiant skin.
