About V3 Derma Clinic

Your Trusted Skin, Hair & Laser Clinic in Champapet, Hyderabad

Our Story

V3 Derma Clinic was established with a vision to bring affordable, world-class dermatology care to the people of Champapet and surrounding areas in Hyderabad. We believe that every individual deserves access to expert skin care backed by scientific evidence and the latest advancements in dermatology.

Our treatments follow evidence-based protocols aligned with international dermatology standards. From common skin conditions to advanced cosmetic procedures, we offer a comprehensive range of services under one roof.

Starting with clinical dermatology, we have expanded our scope to include trichology (hair and scalp treatments), laser therapy, and aesthetic procedures — making us a one-stop destination for all your skin, hair and beauty needs.

Meet Our Doctor

Consultant Dermatologist, Cosmetologist & Trichologist

Our lead dermatologist brings years of dedicated experience in clinical and cosmetic dermatology. With specialised training in laser procedures, chemical peels, PRP therapy and aesthetic treatments, our doctor is committed to delivering personalised, effective care for every patient.
